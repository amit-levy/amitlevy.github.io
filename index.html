About Me

I am a historian of the modern Middle East, specializing in the history of knowledge, Oriental Studies, and German-Jewish cultural transfers to Palestine/Israel.

My research explores how academic traditions migrated and transformed during the 20th century, with a focus on the social and cultural history of the British Mandate and the early State of Israel.
